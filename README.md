**Essential Moments** is an incredibly powerful, privacy-first Android gallery application designed to completely revolutionize how you find and interact with your personal memories. We leverage cutting-edge on-device Machine Learning to enable magical semantic search through all your photos! 🪷

Instead of manually scrolling or searching by boring tags or dates, you can now search your gallery using natural language. Try searching for *"a fluffy dog running in the park"*, *"a cozy rainy evening"*, or *"delicious pasta dinner"*. 🦦

And the best part? All of this magic happens locally on your device! **No internet connection or cloud servers are ever required**, which means your precious memories remain 100% private and secure. 🐢

---

## 🫧 Magical Features

*   🔍 **Semantic Search**: Find your photos instantly using natural language queries powered by insanely advanced vision-language models!
*   🧠 **On-Device Machine Learning**: We utilize incredibly powerful LiteRT (TensorFlow Lite) and SigLIP2 models to deeply understand your image content completely locally.
*   🔒 **Total Privacy**: Your photos *never* leave your phone. All intelligent embeddings and processing happen right where they belong—on your device!
*   🎨 **Beautiful UI/UX**: Enjoy a stunning, snappy, and delightfully intuitive interface crafted completely from scratch using the modern Jetpack Compose.
*   ⚡ **Smart Background Indexing**: Our intelligent background processing hums along beautifully, indexing your massive photo library without draining your battery or slowing you down.
*   🚀 **Blazing Fast Performance**: Seamlessly integrated with native C++ for lightning-fast, highly-optimized machine learning inference!
